# 🚀 Welcome to Pitch My Startup Idea (where Inspiration meets Action)

### ✨ Our Mission
Pitch My Startup Idea is a social platform designed for thinkers, creators, and curious minds to share ideas, exchange perspectives and provide feedback within a global community. We believe inspiration and ideas can come at any age, and therefore we foster a space where meaningful conversations can happen across every domain of interest.
